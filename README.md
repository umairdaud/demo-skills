# Skills Collection

This repository contains a collection of specialized skills that enhance productivity and automate common tasks. Each skill is designed to replace manual processes, saving time and improving quality.

## Available Skills

### 1. Copywriting Email Replier (`copywriting-email-replier`)

**What it replaces:** Manual email composition and proofreading processes that often result in poorly structured or unprofessional communications.

**Functionality:**
- Crafts effective, professional email replies with appropriate tone and structure
- Provides guidance on email composition with proper formatting and clarity
- Ensures emails meet professional standards and achieve communication goals

**Time Saved:**
- Eliminates the need to think about email structure from scratch
- Reduces time spent on proofreading and revising emails
- Estimated time savings: 5-10 minutes per email

**Quality Improved:**
- Consistent professional tone across all communications
- Proper email structure following best practices
- Reduced risk of miscommunication due to unclear messaging
- Automatic compliance with professional email standards

---

### 2. Day Planner (`day-planner-new`)

**What it replaces:** Manual scheduling on paper, basic calendar apps, or mental planning that lacks structured time allocation.

**Functionality:**
- Creates daily schedules with specific time slots for tasks (HH:MM-HH:MM format)
- Manages tasks with add, view, edit, remove, and clear operations
- Provides time-based organization with automatic sorting
- Saves daily plans in JSON format for persistence

**Time Saved:**
- Quick command-line task management without GUI overhead
- Eliminates need for external calendar applications
- Estimated time savings: 10-15 minutes daily planning time

**Quality Improved:**
- Structured time allocation prevents overbooking
- Visual timeline view improves time awareness
- Automatic conflict detection for overlapping time slots
- Consistent daily planning habits with easy tracking

---

### 3. Insurance Premium Calculator (`insurance-premium-calculator-skill`)

**What it replaces:** Manual calculations on paper or basic calculators for complex insurance premium computations involving multiple variables and regulatory requirements.

**Functionality:**
- Calculates net premiums for engineering policies (Contractors All Risk, Erection Policy, Machinery)
- Handles complex calculations including basic premium, administrative charges (capped at Rs 5000), federal surcharges (16% for Punjab, 15% for Sindh), stamp duties (1%), and stamp charges
- Provides detailed breakdown of all calculation components
- Validates inputs and ensures compliance with regulatory formulas

**Time Saved:**
- Eliminates manual calculation errors and recalculation time
- Automates complex multi-step calculations
- Estimated time savings: 8-12 minutes per calculation compared to manual methods

**Quality Improved:**
- Accurate calculations with consistent application of formulas
- Detailed audit trail showing each calculation step
- Compliance with regulatory requirements and provincial differences
- Error prevention through input validation and automated processing
