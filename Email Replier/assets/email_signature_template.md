## Guidelines for Email Signatures

### Do's:
- Keep it concise (4-6 lines max)
- Include only essential contact information
- Match your company's branding standards
- Update regularly when information changes
- Use plain text when possible for compatibility

### Don'ts:
- Don't include large graphics or images
- Don't overcrowd with too much information
- Don't use animated GIFs or flashing text
- Don't include controversial quotes or images
- Don't make it longer than necessary
